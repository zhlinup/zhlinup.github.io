About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Data Science, Lingnan University (LU). Before that, I was a Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I received my Ph.D. degree in Information Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2022, and my B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2017. My research interests include spatial perception and immersive communications, GenAI for communications, distributed and federated learning, and edge AI systems.
