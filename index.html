About Me

I am currently a VPRDO Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I received my Ph.D. degree in Information Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2022, and my B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2017. My research interests include spatial intelligence, holographic communication, neural data representation and compression, distributed learning and foundation models, and edge AI systems.

Research Interests

  • Spatial Intelligence

  • Holographic Communication

  • Neural Data Representation and Compression

  • Distributed Learning and Foundation Models

  • O-RAN and Edge AI Systems
